About Us

A Foundation Built
on Purpose

Monroe InspirEd Foundation was founded by a family united by a shared calling — to invest in the next generation of educators. What began as a deeply personal commitment grew into a formal organization dedicated to removing barriers, building confidence, and illuminating the path toward a career in teaching.

We believe that the best educators are those who were inspired by someone who believed in them first. That belief is at the heart of everything we do — from the scholarships we award to the students we coach and tutor along the way.

Our programs are designed to meet students where they are and walk with them toward where they want to be.

Our Mission

Monroe InspirEd is dedicated to supporting and empowering students who aspire to become educators by providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities that inspire leadership, equity, and excellence in education.

What We Offer

Our Programs

Tomorrow's Teachers Scholarship

Supporting aspiring educators with financial resources to pursue their dream of teaching. We invest in students who have the heart and the drive to lead the next generation.

Overseen by Terrence Bomar

Tutoring Services

Personalized academic support for students at all levels. Our tutoring program meets students where they are — building skills, restoring confidence, and unlocking academic potential.

Overseen by Stefani Bomar

Life Coaching

Guiding students toward clarity, purpose, and personal growth. Our certified life coach works with students to help them discover direction, navigate challenges, and step into their full potential.

Overseen by Gail Francis
